The Story

One-Eyed Wonder

Jarred Crabtree is a 23-year-old footballer from Waubra, Victoria, who has become a local legend despite losing sight in his right eye as an infant due to a cataract.

His left eye has shouldered the full workload for years and is now deteriorating, requiring glasses off the field and contact lenses during games. Yet this hasn't stopped him from excelling on the footy field.

In the 2018 season with the Waubra Emus in the Central Highlands Football League, Jarred kicked an impressive 30 goals in just 12 games and earned selection to represent the league in the AFL Victoria Community Championships.

From early days with Noradjuha-Quantong and Minyip-Murtoa, through tougher times at Skipton, his move to Waubra marked a turning point.
